What makes our diamonds so special?

Our diamonds are lab grown which makes them conflict-free and also leave minimal carbon footprint compared to traditional mined diamonds. Lab grown and mined diamonds are basically the same and can't be told apart except by laboratories. Lab grown diamonds are not fake diamonds, they are created in a simulated and controlled environment as natural diamonds are and thus are in every sense a real diamond.
